# 🍎 The Smart Kitchen Helper

Hi there! I made some changes to our kitchen to make sure the food stays super yummy and fresh for a long time. Here is how it works, explained just for you!

## 🏠 Our Kitchen Tables
Imagine our kitchen has three special tables:
1.  **🔥 The Red Table:** This is for hot things, like yummy pizza! It keeps them nice and warm.
2.  **❄️ The Blue Table:** This is for cold things, like ice cream! It keeps them nice and chilly.
3.  **🧺 The Yellow Table (The Shelf):** This is for everything else, like bread. But there is a secret: if we put pizza or ice cream on the Yellow Table, it starts to go bad **twice as fast** because it's not on its favorite table!

## 🚀 The Problem
Sometimes, the Red Table or the Blue Table gets too full. When that happens, we have to put our pizza or ice cream on the Yellow Table. Oh no! We don't want them to go bad!

## ✨ My Smart Change: "The Helping Hand"
Before, once we put the pizza on the Yellow Table, it just sat there getting bad quickly. But I taught our kitchen a new trick:

**"Whenever someone comes and picks up a pizza from the Red Table, we immediately check the Yellow Table. If we see another pizza waiting there, we grab it and move it to the Red Table right away!"**

### Why is this better?
*   **Freshness Power!** ⚡ Food stays fresh longer because it spends more time on its favorite table.
*   **No Waste!** 🗑️ We don't have to throw away as much food because we are being super smart helpers.
*   **Happy Customers!** 😊 Everyone gets their food when it's still yummy and not "yucky."

## 🛠️ What I Changed in the Code
I added a special part called `tryMoveToIdeal`. It's like a little robot that:
1.  Waits for a spot to open up on the Red or Blue table.
2.  Looks at the Yellow Table (The Shelf).
3.  If it finds a "matching" food, it moves it to its favorite table!

That's it! Now our kitchen is the smartest kitchen in the world! 🌍👨‍🍳
